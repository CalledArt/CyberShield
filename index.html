Phishing

Phishing

Fake emails that trick users into giving credentials or clicking malicious links.

Prevention Tips:

  • Verify sender email addresses
  • Don't click suspicious links
  • Check for spelling errors
  • Use two-factor authentication

Malware

Malware

Harmful software that damages systems, steals data, or gains unauthorized access.

Prevention Tips:

  • Keep software updated
  • Use reputable antivirus
  • Don't download from unknown sources
  • Be cautious with email attachments

Ransomware

Ransomware

Locks your files until a ransom is paid, often encrypting data to make it inaccessible.

Prevention Tips:

  • Regular data backups
  • Keep systems updated
  • Use strong passwords
  • Never pay the ransom

DDoS

DDoS Attack

Floods a server with traffic to crash it or make it unavailable to legitimate users.

Prevention Tips:

  • Use DDoS protection services
  • Monitor network traffic
  • Implement rate limiting
  • Have backup servers ready

Social Engineering

Social Engineering

Manipulating people to reveal confidential information or perform actions that compromise security.

Prevention Tips:

  • Verify identity before sharing info
  • Be skeptical of urgent requests
  • Train employees regularly
  • Follow security protocols

Password Attacks

Password Attacks

Attempts to crack or steal passwords to gain unauthorized access to accounts and systems.

Prevention Tips:

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Use a password manager
  • Change passwords regularly
